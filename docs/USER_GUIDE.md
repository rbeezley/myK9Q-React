# myK9Q User Guide

A complete guide to using the myK9Q dog show scoring app.

---

## Getting Started

### How do I log in?
Enter the 5-character passcode provided by the show organizers. The passcode determines your role:
- Starts with **a** = Admin
- Starts with **j** = Judge
- Starts with **s** = Steward
- Starts with **e** = Exhibitor

After logging in, the app downloads your show data for offline use.

### What can I do with my role?

| Feature | Exhibitor | Steward | Judge | Admin |
|---------|-----------|---------|-------|-------|
| Check in dogs | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Change run order | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Score entries | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Manage classes | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Admin settings | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

### How do I install the app?
myK9Q is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On your phone:
- **iPhone**: Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
- **Android**: Tap the menu → "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"

## Finding Dogs & Classes

### How do I find my dog?
From the **Home** screen:
1. Use the search bar to type your dog's name, breed, or handler name
2. Tap on your dog's card to see their details
3. Tap the heart icon to add them to your favorites

### How do I see what classes my dog is in?
Tap on your dog's armband number from the Home screen. The Dog Details page shows all classes that dog is entered in.

### How do I find a specific class?
1. Tap the hamburger menu (☰) → **Classes**
2. Or from Home, tap a trial card to see its classes
3. Use the search bar to find classes by name or element

### What do the class status badges mean?
- **Setup** - Class is being prepared
- **Briefing** - Judge is giving instructions
- **Break** - Class is on break
- **In Progress** - Dogs are currently competing
- **Completed** - All dogs have been scored

## Check-In

### How do I check in my dog?
**Option 1 - From Home:**
1. Find your dog on the Home screen
2. Tap the trial card where your dog is entered
3. Tap the class → Entry List
4. Find your dog and tap the status button
5. Select "Checked In"

**Option 2 - From Dog Details:**
1. Tap your dog's armband on the Home screen
2. Find the class card
3. Tap the check-in button

### What do the entry status colors mean?
- **Gray** - No status (not checked in yet)
- **Teal** - Checked in and ready to compete
- **Orange** - Come to Gate (steward is calling you)
- **Purple** - At Gate (waiting at ring entrance)
- **Blue** - In Ring (actively competing)
- **Amber** - Conflict (overlapping classes)
- **Red** - Pulled (withdrawn from class)
- **Green** - Completed (scored)

### What do the class status colors mean?
- **Gray** - No status
- **Brown** - Setup
- **Orange** - Briefing
- **Purple** - Break
- **Teal** - Start Time set
- **Blue** - In Progress
- **Green** - Completed

### What does "Conflict" status mean?
A conflict means your dog is entered in multiple classes that may be running at the same time. Check with the steward about which class to go to first.

### Can I check in multiple dogs at once?
Stewards and judges can use "Check-in All Pending" to check in all dogs in a class at once. Exhibitors check in one dog at a time.

## Viewing Results & Statistics

### How do I see my dog's scores?
1. Tap on your dog from the Home screen
2. The Dog Details page shows all entries with scores
3. Completed classes show the score, time, and qualification status

### How do I see overall show results?
1. Tap the hamburger menu (☰) → **The Podium**
2. View the podium showing 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place dogs
3. Filter by trial, element, or level

### How do I see statistics?
1. Tap the hamburger menu (☰) → **Statistics**
2. View qualification rates, fastest times, and breed performance
3. Use filters to drill down by trial, element, level, or breed

### Where can I see fastest times?
Go to **Statistics** and scroll down to the "Fastest Times" table. This shows the top performers ranked by completion time.

## Favorites & Notifications

### How do I favorite a dog?
Tap the heart icon on any dog card. Favorited dogs appear at the top when you select the "Favorites" tab.

### What notifications can I receive?
- **Podium placement** - When your favorited dog places 1st, 2nd, or 3rd
- **Up soon** - When the dog ahead of yours finishes
- **Come to gate** - When the steward calls your dog

### How do I enable notifications?
1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Enable push notifications
3. Allow notification permission when your browser asks

## For Judges

### How do I score a dog?
1. Navigate to the class's Entry List
2. Find the dog and tap the **scoresheet icon** (clipboard)
3. Enter the score, time, and qualification status
4. Tap **Submit Score**
5. Confirm the score in the dialog

### How do I change the run order?
1. On the Entry List, tap the **Run Order** button
2. Drag entries up or down to reorder
3. Or use preset options: Randomize, Reverse, By Armband
4. Tap **Apply**

### How do I mark a dog "In Ring"?
Tap the dog's status button and select "In Ring". This moves them to the top of the list and helps exhibitors know who's competing.

### Can I score offline?
Yes! All scoring works offline. Scores are saved locally and automatically sync when you reconnect to the internet. You'll see a sync indicator showing pending scores.

### How do I print reports?
1. On the Entry List, tap the three-dot menu (⋯)
2. Choose from:
   - **Check-in Sheet** - List of dogs with check-in status
   - **Results Sheet** - Scores and placements
   - **Scoresheet Report** - Formatted scoresheets

## For Stewards

### How do I manage the gate?
1. Go to the class's Entry List
2. When a dog arrives at the gate, tap their status → "At Gate"
3. When it's their turn, change to "In Ring"
4. When done, the judge will mark them completed

### How do I call a dog to the gate?
Change their status to "Come to Gate". If the exhibitor has notifications enabled, they'll receive an alert.

### How do I handle a scratched dog?
Tap the dog's status and select "Pulled". This removes them from the active run order.

## For Admins

### How do I access admin settings?
Tap the hamburger menu (☰) → **Results Control** (only visible to admin users)

### What can I configure in Results Control?
- **Result visibility** - Control when results are shown (immediately, after class, after review)
- **Self check-in** - Allow/disallow exhibitor self check-in per class
- **Live results** - Enable/disable real-time result updates

### How do I view the audit log?
In **Results Control**, tap the menu icon (⋮) → **Audit Log** to see all scoring actions, who made them, and when.

## Offline Mode

### Does the app work offline?
Yes! After logging in, all your show data is stored on your device. You can:
- View dogs, classes, and entries
- Check in dogs
- Score entries (judges/admins)

Changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

### How do I know if I'm offline?
Look for the Wi-Fi icon in the header. If you see a crossed-out Wi-Fi symbol and "Offline" text, you're offline.

### Why can't I log out?
If you have pending scores that haven't synced, you'll need to wait for them to upload before logging out. This prevents losing any scores.

### How do I force a sync?
1. Pull down on any list to refresh
2. Or tap the refresh icon in the header
3. Or go to **Settings** → **Sync Now**

## Settings

### How do I change to dark mode?
Go to **Settings** → **Appearance** → Toggle **Dark Mode**

### How do I export my data?
Go to **Settings** → **Advanced** → **Export My Data** (Admin only)

### How do I clear cached data?
Go to **Settings** → **Advanced** → **Clear All Data** (Admin only). Note: This will require re-downloading show data on next login.

## Troubleshooting

### The app seems stuck or frozen
1. Pull down to refresh
2. If that doesn't work, close and reopen the app
3. Check your internet connection

### My scores aren't showing up
1. Check if you're online (Wi-Fi icon in header)
2. Look for the sync indicator - pending scores will upload automatically
3. Pull down to refresh after reconnecting

### I can't find my dog
1. Make sure you're logged in with the correct passcode
2. Use the search bar on the Home screen
3. Check the "All Dogs" tab (not just Favorites)

### Push notifications aren't working
1. Check **Settings** → **Notifications** is enabled
2. Make sure you allowed notification permission in your browser
3. On iPhone, notifications only work if you installed the app to your home screen

### The run order looks wrong
Run order can be customized by judges/stewards. If you think there's an error, check with the ring steward.

## Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Armband** | The unique number assigned to your dog for the show |
| **Call Name** | Your dog's everyday name (not their registered name) |
| **Check-in** | Confirming your dog is present and ready to compete |
| **Run Order** | The sequence in which dogs will compete |
| **Element** | The type of competition (Scent Work, Rally, Obedience, etc.) |
| **Level** | The difficulty tier (Novice, Open, Excellent, Masters, etc.) |
| **Q / Qualifying** | Your dog passed and earned a qualifying score |
| **NQ** | Did not qualify (below passing threshold) |
| **Placement** | Final ranking (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.) |
| **License Key** | The unique identifier for your show |
| **Passcode** | Your 5-character login code |
